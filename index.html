About Me

Hello there! I'm a passionate game developer and web designer dedicated to crafting interactive, meaningful, and immersive digital experiences. My work centers on blending creativity with technology to deliver engaging user experiences across various platforms, with a particular focus on games and websites.

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Miami, complemented by minors in Mathematics and Interactive Media. Through both my academic journey and my roles as a student developer in cross-functional teams and a Teaching Assistant for two Computer Science courses, I've honed both my technical expertise and collaborative skills.

When I'm not working, I love expressing myself through music—whether it's belting out dramatic songs or playing the piano. I also love exploring narrative-rich games and new crochet projects.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see how I merge creativity with technology to build meaningful digital experiences.
